Retirement Withdrawal Tax Impact
Estimated annual tax burden based on withdrawal source at your effective rate
Tax bucket notes: Tax-free accounts (Roth IRA, HSA) provide tax-free growth and tax-free withdrawals in retirement. Tax-deferred accounts (401k, 403b, traditional IRA, TSP) offer tax deductions now but withdrawals are taxed as ordinary income. After-tax accounts (brokerage, taxable accounts) are funded with after-tax dollars; only gains are taxed (typically at capital gains rates). This model applies your specified effective rate to tax-deferred withdrawals and 50% of that rate to after-tax account gains as an approximation. Actual tax impact depends on your specific situation. Consult a tax professional.
